Core Work

Research Focus

Neurophysiological Assessment

Combining surface EMG, transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS), and motor nerve stimulation to create objective assessment tools for isometric strength testing in para athletes.

Para Sport Classification

Developing evidence-based protocols to detect intentional misrepresentation of effort during classification, contributing to fair and transparent Paralympic sport systems worldwide.

Signal Processing & Modelling

Custom MATLAB algorithm development for real-time EMG signal analysis, force-matching paradigm control, and advanced statistical modelling (R, Python, SPSS).

Teaching & Mentorship

Delivering neurophysiology and exercise science to 200+ undergraduates with a 4.75/5.0 student rating. Supervising 4 Honours projects and 20+ Master's students across two universities.
