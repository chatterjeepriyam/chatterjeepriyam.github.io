Research Focus

I investigate neuromuscular function using neurophysiological measurements, with a focus on identifying objective markers of Intentional Misrepresentation of efforts in strength testing. My work in para-athletes, contributing toward more robust and evidence-based Para sport classification systems.

Why It Matters

Current para-sport classification systems rely heavily on subjective assessments. My work aims to introduce objective, evidence-based approaches using physiological signals and quantitative modeling.

Research Areas

  • Surface EMG and signal processing
  • Motor control and neuromuscular assessment
  • Force control and performance analysis
  • Detection of intentional misrepresentation
